# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone participating in this project.

All contributors, regardless of experience, background, or identity, are expected to treat others with respect and professionalism.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

* Be respectful and courteous.
* Provide constructive feedback.
* Welcome new contributors.
* Focus on improving the project.
* Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
* Communicate professionally.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

* Harassment or discrimination.
* Personal attacks or insults.
* Offensive or inappropriate language.
* Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior.
* Sharing private or confidential information without permission.
* Any conduct that negatively impacts the project community.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation from individuals who violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a GitHub issue or contact the project maintainer.

All reports will be reviewed fairly and handled appropriately.
